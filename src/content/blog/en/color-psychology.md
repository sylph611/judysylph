## What is Color Psychology?

Color psychology studies how colors affect human emotions, behavior, and decision-making. It's applied in marketing, interior design, and many other fields.

## Psychological Effects of Major Colors

### 1. Red

- Passion, energy, urgency
- Increases appetite, raises heart rate
- **Uses**: CTA buttons, sale signs

### 2. Blue

- Trust, stability, peace
- Improves productivity, calms the mind
- **Uses**: Corporate branding, bedroom decor

### 3. Yellow

- Happiness, optimism, creativity
- Enhances attention, elevates mood
- **Uses**: Warning signs, children's spaces

### 4. Green

- Nature, growth, balance
- Reduces stress, relieves eye strain
- **Uses**: Eco brands, relaxation spaces

### 5. Purple

- Creativity, luxury, mystery
- Stimulates imagination
- **Uses**: Beauty brands, meditation spaces

## Using Colors in Daily Life

- **Workspace**: Blue for better focus
- **Exercise area**: Red for energy boost
- **Rest area**: Green or blue for relaxation
- **Creative space**: Yellow or purple for inspiration

You can also understand your current mental state through your favorite colors!
