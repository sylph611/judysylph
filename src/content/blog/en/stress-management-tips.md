## Why Manage Stress?

Moderate stress can be motivating, but chronic stress harms both physical and mental health.

## 10 Stress Management Tips

### 1. 4-7-8 Breathing
Inhale 4 seconds, hold 7 seconds, exhale 8 seconds

### 2. Regular Exercise
Even 30 minutes of walking helps

### 3. Quality Sleep
7-8 hours of good sleep

### 4. Digital Detox
Turn off phone 1 hour before bed

### 5. Nature Time
Walk in parks, grow plants

### 6. Hobbies
Spend time on activities you love

### 7. Social Connection
Talk with people you care about

### 8. Mindfulness
Focus on the present moment

### 9. Set Boundaries
Practice saying "no"

### 10. Gratitude Journal
Write 3 things you're grateful for daily

## Start Today

You don't need to try everything at once. Apply them one by one to your daily life.
