## What Are Love Languages?

Dr. Gary Chapman explains that people express and receive love in 5 different ways.

## The 5 Love Languages

### 1. Words of Affirmation

- Praise, encouragement, gratitude
- "I'm proud of you", "I appreciate you"

### 2. Quality Time

- Undivided attention
- Conversations, shared activities

### 3. Receiving Gifts

- Thoughtful, meaningful presents
- It's the thought that counts

### 4. Acts of Service

- Actions that help
- Chores, running errands

### 5. Physical Touch

- Physical contact
- Holding hands, hugs, pats

## Applying in Relationships

- Identify your primary love language
- Discover your partner's love language
- Express love in the way they prefer
