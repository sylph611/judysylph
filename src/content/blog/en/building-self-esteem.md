## What is Self-Esteem?

Self-esteem is how much you value yourself and believe you deserve love and respect. Healthy self-esteem is the foundation of a happy life.

## Signs of Low Self-Esteem

- Harsh self-criticism over small mistakes
- Overly sensitive to others' opinions
- Fear of new challenges
- Difficulty expressing your own views

### 2. Acknowledge Small Wins

- Write down 3 things you accomplished daily
- Praise yourself, no matter how small

### 3. Stop Comparing

- Reduce social media time
- Only compare yourself to yesterday's you

### 4. Set Boundaries

- Say no when you mean no
- Protect your time and energy

### 5. Take Care of Your Body

- Regular exercise
- Adequate sleep and nutrition

### 6. Focus on Strengths

- List things you're good at
- Find opportunities to use your strengths

### 7. Practice Self-Compassion

- Be kind to yourself when you fail
- "It's okay, everyone makes mistakes"

## Remember

Self-esteem doesn't change overnight. With daily practice, you'll gradually discover a more confident you.
